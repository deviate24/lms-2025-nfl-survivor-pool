NFL Survivor Pool Rules

Basic Concept

The NFL Survivor Pool is a season-long competition where participants pick one NFL team each week to win. The key twist: you can only use each team once during the entire season.

How to Play

  1. Each week, select one NFL team you believe will win their game.
  2. If your selected team wins, you "survive" and continue to the next week.
  3. If your selected team loses or ties, you're eliminated from the pool.
  4. You cannot pick the same team twice in a season.
  5. All picks must be submitted before the weekly deadline (typically Thursday at 4:00 PM PT for the first game of the week).

Special Weeks

Some weeks may be designated as "Double-Pick" weeks:

  • You must select two different teams to win.
  • You need both picks to be correct to automatically advance.
  • If no entries pick both teams correctly, then entries with one correct pick will advance.
  • If multiple entries have the same number of correct picks (1 out of 2), they all advance to the next week.
  • If both of your picks lose or tie, you're eliminated regardless of how others performed.
  • Remember, you still can't reuse any teams from previous weeks.

Winning the Pool

The last participant(s) standing wins the pool. If multiple participants survive until the end of the regular season, the prize will be split equally among them.

Tiebreakers

If all remaining participants are eliminated in the same week:

  1. The participant who correctly picked the most weeks overall wins.
  2. If still tied, the prize is split equally.

Deadlines

All picks must be submitted before the weekly deadline. The deadline is typically set to Thursday at 4:00 PM PT, or 10 minutes before the first game of the week (whichever comes first).

Important: Missing a deadline counts as an elimination. No exceptions will be made for late picks.

Privacy Policy

To maintain fairness, current week picks are hidden from other participants until the weekly deadline passes. This prevents players from gaining an advantage by seeing others' selections.

Pool Entries

Participants may have multiple entries in the pool. Each entry:

  • Must make independent picks
  • Is tracked separately for elimination
  • Requires separate entry fees

Contact

If you have any questions about the rules or need clarification, please contact the pool administrator.
